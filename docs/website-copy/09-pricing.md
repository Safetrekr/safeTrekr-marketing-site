## Section 6: FAQ

### Q: Is there a minimum commitment or annual contract?

**A:** No. You can submit a single trip with no ongoing commitment. Volume discounts are available for organizations that want annual agreements, but they're optional.

### Q: How do you calculate per-participant cost?

**A:** Per-participant cost depends on group size. A $450 Day Trip for 30 participants is $15/person. The same trip for 15 participants is $30/person. Pricing is per trip, not per participant.

### Q: Can we see SafeTrekr before submitting a trip?

**A:** Yes. Schedule a walkthrough to see a complete safety binder for your organization type. We'll show you exactly what you receive before you submit your first trip.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?

**A:** We accept credit cards for individual trips. Organizations with volume agreements can pay by invoice.

### Q: Do you offer nonprofit or educational pricing?

**A:** Contact us to discuss pricing for nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. We work with organizations of all sizes to find arrangements that work.

### Q: What happens if we need to cancel a trip after submitting?

**A:** If you cancel before the analyst review begins, you receive a full refund. If the review has started, we'll provide a credit for a future trip.
