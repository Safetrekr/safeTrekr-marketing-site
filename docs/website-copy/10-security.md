## Section 2: Security Practices

**Eyebrow:** SECURITY PRACTICES

**Headline:** How we handle your data.

### Practice 1: Encryption

**Description:** All data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption. Data in transit is protected by TLS 1.3. Your information is secured both when stored and when transmitted.

### Practice 2: Role-Based Access Controls

**Description:** Access to traveler data is controlled by role. Trip coordinators see their trips. Administrators see organizational data. Analysts see only what they need for review. No one has access they don't need.

### Practice 3: Verified Documentation

**Description:** Every finding in your safety binder is recorded with integrity verification. Documentation demonstrates that content has not been modified after completion. Professional records you can rely on.

### Practice 4: Data Minimization

**Description:** We collect only the information necessary for safety assessment. No unnecessary data collection. No secondary use. No selling or sharing of traveler information.

### Practice 5: Secure Infrastructure

**Description:** SafeTrekr runs on cloud infrastructure with strong security practices. Redundant systems, regular backups, and monitoring help protect availability.

### Practice 6: Regular Assessment

**Description:** We conduct regular security assessments to validate our practices and identify areas for improvement.

## Section 4: Data Practices

**Eyebrow:** DATA PRACTICES

**Headline:** What we do -- and don't do -- with your data.

**We Do:**
- Encrypt all data at rest and in transit
- Limit access to only those who need it
- Retain data only as long as necessary
- Provide data export and deletion upon request
- Maintain logs of access
- Conduct regular security assessments

**We Don't:**
- Sell or share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising or marketing
- Retain data indefinitely after trip completion
- Access your data without legitimate purpose
- Store unnecessary personal information
