## Section 5: FAQ

### Q: How long is the walkthrough?

**A:** Most walkthroughs run 30 minutes. We can go longer if you have detailed questions.

### Q: Who should attend?

**A:** Anyone involved in travel planning or safety decisions. We often meet with administrators, trip coordinators, athletic directors, mission coordinators, or HR leaders.

### Q: Is there any obligation after the walkthrough?

**A:** No. The walkthrough is an opportunity for you to see if SafeTrekr is right for your organization. No pressure.

### Q: Can we see a safety binder for a specific destination?

**A:** Yes. Let us know the destinations you're considering and we'll show you relevant examples.
