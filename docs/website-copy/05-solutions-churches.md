## Section 6: FAQ

### Q: Does SafeTrekr work with church insurance requirements?

**A:** Yes. Many church insurance carriers appreciate documented safety planning for sponsored travel -- especially international missions and youth trips. SafeTrekr safety binders provide the organized documentation that carriers want to see. The structured format demonstrates that preparation was completed before travel, not created afterward.

### Q: How do you handle mission trips to challenging regions?

**A:** Our International and Challenging Region tiers include enhanced regional assessment, evacuation planning documentation, and embassy contact verification. For regions with travel advisories, we provide context and documented considerations. We help you make informed decisions about ministry travel -- we don't make those decisions for you.

### Q: Can we share the safety binder with parents?

**A:** Absolutely. Safety binders are designed to be shared with parents, elders, denominational leadership, and insurance carriers. When parents ask "How did you prepare for this trip?" you can share professional documentation -- not just "We've done this before."

### Q: Our volunteer leaders aren't technical. Is SafeTrekr complicated to use?

**A:** SafeTrekr was designed for volunteer leaders with day jobs and families. Submitting a trip takes about 15 minutes through a guided form -- just enter your destination, dates, team size, and planned activities. No training required. Your volunteers receive a completed safety binder with clear checklists and guidance.

### Q: What if our ministry partner changes or our itinerary shifts?

**A:** If your destination or primary ministry partner changes significantly, we recommend submitting updated trip details for review. Minor itinerary adjustments to the same destination can be accommodated -- contact your analyst for guidance.

### Q: Do you offer pricing for churches that do multiple trips per year?

**A:** Yes. Volume pricing is available for churches with regular mission programs. Contact us to discuss arrangements that fit your ministry's travel patterns.
