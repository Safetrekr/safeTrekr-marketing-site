## Section 2: FAQ Categories

### Category 1: Getting Started

#### Q: How do I submit my first trip?

**A:** After creating your account, you'll access a guided submission form that collects destination, dates, participants, activities, and logistics. The form takes approximately 15 minutes to complete. No training or special knowledge required -- just the details of your planned trip.

#### Q: How long does it take to receive documentation?

**A:** Standard delivery is 3-5 business days from trip submission. Extended Trip and International tiers receive priority analyst assignment. Expedited 48-hour delivery is available as an add-on.

#### Q: Is there a minimum commitment or contract?

**A:** No. You can submit a single trip with no ongoing commitment. Volume discounts are available for organizations that want annual agreements, but they're optional.

#### Q: What if I need to change my trip details after submitting?

**A:** Minor changes to dates or logistics can be accommodated -- contact your analyst. Significant changes (new destination, different venues) may require a new submission.

#### Q: Can I see a sample safety binder before purchasing?

**A:** Yes. Schedule a walkthrough and we'll show you a complete safety binder for your organization type. You can also view sample binders from our resources page.

### Category 2: The Safety Binder

#### Q: What's included in a safety binder?

**A:** Every safety binder includes: executive summary, complete 17-section analyst findings, structured assessment, emergency contact directory, maps and evacuation routes, analyst recommendations, data source citations, and integrity verification.

#### Q: How do I share the safety binder with stakeholders?

**A:** Safety binders can be exported as PDF for offline sharing. You can also generate shareable links with configurable access permissions. Share with board members, insurance carriers, parents, or anyone who needs to review your preparation.

#### Q: What does "verified documentation" mean?

**A:** Every finding in your safety binder is recorded with integrity verification. This demonstrates that the document has not been altered after completion. Professional documentation that stakeholders can trust.

#### Q: Can I use the safety binder for insurance purposes?

**A:** Yes. The safety binder documents that professional assessment was conducted -- exactly what insurance carriers appreciate seeing. Many organizations share binders with their insurance carrier as evidence of preparation.

#### Q: How long do I have access to my safety binder?

**A:** Access duration depends on your tier: Day Trip (30 days post-trip), Extended Trip (60 days), International (90 days). Binders can be downloaded as PDF for permanent records.

### Category 3: Safety Information

#### Q: What government data sources do you use?

**A:** We gather information from five government sources: NOAA (weather and environmental), USGS (geological), CDC (health), GDACS (alerts), and ReliefWeb (regional conditions). These are the same sources used by professionals who assess conditions.

#### Q: How is the information assessed?

**A:** We use structured assessment methodology that evaluates conditions in context rather than providing simple pass/fail flags. You see the full picture, not just binary indicators.

#### Q: How current is the information?

**A:** Information is gathered at the time of analyst review and reflects current conditions. For active trips with monitoring, briefings are provided as conditions evolve.

#### Q: Can I request an assessment for a destination before booking travel?

**A:** The safety binder is designed for planned trips with specific dates and logistics. For pre-planning destination research, contact us to discuss options.

### Category 4: For Schools

#### Q: Is SafeTrekr designed for student privacy?

**A:** Yes. SafeTrekr is built with student data protection in mind. We use role-based access controls, encryption, data minimization practices, and do not sell or share student data. Schools can request documentation for district procurement.

#### Q: Can multiple schools in our district use SafeTrekr?

**A:** Yes. SafeTrekr supports district-wide use with centralized administration. District administrators can view all trips across schools, establish consistent processes, and access documentation.

#### Q: How do chaperones access trip information during travel?

**A:** Chaperones download the SafeTrekr mobile app and receive a trip code. The app provides offline access to emergency contacts, rally points, medical facilities, and check-in tools.

#### Q: What if a parent asks to see the safety binder?

**A:** Safety binders are designed to be shared. Many schools proactively share binders with parents to demonstrate preparation and build confidence.

### Category 5: For Churches

#### Q: How does SafeTrekr help with mission trip preparation?

**A:** SafeTrekr provides professional assessment for international missions, including embassy contacts, medical facility locations, regional conditions, and emergency planning. Safety binders document that professional preparation was completed -- valuable for insurance carriers and denominational requirements.

#### Q: Do you work with mission organizations and partners abroad?

**A:** We assess the overall destination and can incorporate information about established mission partners you're working with. Partner context is included in International tier reviews.

#### Q: Is SafeTrekr affordable for small churches?

**A:** Yes. At $450 for a domestic trip and $750 for international, SafeTrekr is accessible for churches of any size. For a 30-person mission team, that's $15-25 per person.

### Category 6: For Corporate

#### Q: What is "duty of care" for employee travel?

**A:** Duty of care is the responsibility employers have to take reasonable steps to prepare for employee safety during work activities, including business travel. SafeTrekr provides documented evidence that your company conducted professional assessment -- demonstrating responsible preparation.

#### Q: We're a mid-market company without a travel risk team. Is SafeTrekr right for us?

**A:** Yes -- that's exactly who SafeTrekr is built for. Larger corporations have dedicated travel risk teams. SafeTrekr brings the same professional approach to mid-market companies without dedicated risk staff.

#### Q: Can SafeTrekr work with our travel booking system?

**A:** SafeTrekr can complement existing travel workflows. Contact us for information about integration options.

### Category 7: Security & Privacy

#### Q: How is my data protected?

**A:** All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Role-based access controls limit data visibility. We maintain access logs and conduct regular security assessments.

#### Q: Do you sell or share my data?

**A:** No. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising. Data is used only for providing trip planning services.

#### Q: What security practices do you follow?

**A:** SafeTrekr is built with thoughtful security practices. Security information is available for organizations during procurement discussions.

#### Q: How long do you retain my data?

**A:** Active trip data is retained for the trip duration plus your post-trip access period. Completed safety binders are retained per your organization's requirements. Organizations can discuss custom retention periods.

### Category 8: Billing & Support

#### Q: What payment methods do you accept?

**A:** We accept credit cards for individual trips. Organizations with volume agreements can pay by invoice.

#### Q: What if I need to cancel a trip?

**A:** If you cancel before analyst review begins, you receive a full refund. If review has started, we'll provide a credit for a future trip.

#### Q: How do I get help with my account or active trips?

**A:** Email support@safetrekr.com for assistance with your account or active trips. For sales questions, contact sales@safetrekr.com.

#### Q: Is there a phone number for support?

**A:** Primary support is via email for documentation and tracking purposes. Phone support is available for organizations with support agreements.
