## Section 2: Process Timeline

**Headline:** The SafeTrekr Process

### Step 1: Share Your Trip Details

**Timeline:** Day 1 (15 minutes)

**Description:** Enter your destination, dates, participants, activities, and logistics through a guided submission form. No specialized training required. The form collects everything our analysts need to begin the review.

**What You Provide:**
- Destination and travel dates
- Number and composition of travelers
- Planned activities and venues
- Transportation arrangements
- Lodging details
- Any known concerns or special requirements

### Step 2: Information Gathering

**Timeline:** Days 1-2

**Description:** Our system gathers current safety information from 5 government data sources for your specific destination and travel dates. Weather patterns, geological considerations, health advisories, alerts, and regional conditions -- all specific to where and when your team will be.

**Data Sources:**
- NOAA (weather and environmental data)
- USGS (geological considerations)
- CDC (health advisories and information)
- GDACS (alerts and emergency information)
- ReliefWeb (regional conditions)

### Step 3: Analyst Review

**Timeline:** Days 2-4

**Description:** A trained safety analyst conducts a comprehensive 17-section review of your trip. Every venue, every transportation leg, every activity is evaluated. The analyst applies professional judgment informed by current information -- not automated data processing.

**17 Review Sections:**
- Destination overview
- Venue safety assessment
- Lodging evaluation
- Transportation assessment
- Emergency medical facilities
- Evacuation routes
- Weather and environmental conditions
- Health considerations
- Communication infrastructure
- Local emergency contacts
- Activity-specific considerations
- Food and water considerations
- Accessibility considerations
- Documentation review
- Regulatory considerations
- Historical context
- Final recommendations

### Step 4: Binder Compilation

**Timeline:** Days 4-5

**Description:** Every finding, recommendation, and assessment is compiled into your safety binder. The binder is structured for multiple audiences: trip leaders who need actionable information, administrators who need oversight, and stakeholders who need evidence of preparation.

**Binder Contents:**
- Executive summary
- Complete 17-section findings
- Structured assessment
- Emergency contact directory
- Maps and evacuation routes
- Analyst recommendations
- Data source citations
- Integrity verification

### Step 5: Delivery and Access

**Timeline:** Day 5

**Description:** Your complete safety binder is delivered through the SafeTrekr platform. Download as PDF for presentations, share with stakeholders, and access through the mobile app during travel.

**Delivery Options:**
- Digital access through SafeTrekr platform
- PDF export for offline use
- Mobile app access for field support
- Shareable links for stakeholders
