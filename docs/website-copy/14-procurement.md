## Section 3: Common Questions

**Headline:** Frequently asked procurement questions

### Q: Is SafeTrekr on any cooperative purchasing contracts?

**A:** We are actively exploring cooperative purchasing options. Contact us for current availability in your state or region.

### Q: What is the implementation timeline?

**A:** SafeTrekr requires no implementation project. Organizations can submit their first trip immediately after account creation. There is no training requirement.

### Q: Do you offer pilot programs?

**A:** Yes. Many organizations start with a pilot of 3-5 trips before expanding to organization-wide use. Contact us to discuss pilot pricing.

### Q: What are your payment terms?

**A:** Standard payment terms are net 30 for invoiced accounts. Credit card payment is available for per-trip purchases.

### Q: Do you sign institutional contracts?

**A:** Yes. We work with institutional teams to execute service agreements that meet your requirements.
